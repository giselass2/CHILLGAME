    HOW TO PLAY

    Control the Chillfish to survive the depths!

    Movement:

    • Keyboard (PC): Use the Arrow Keys (↑↓←→) or WASD to move.
    • Touch (Mobile): Touch and drag anywhere on the screen to move the fish.

    Shoot:

    • Keyboard (PC): Press the SPACEBAR to shoot bubbles.
    • Touch (Mobile): Tap anywhere on the screen (outside of dragging for movement) to shoot bubbles.

    Goal:

    • Avoid the pufferfish!
    • Shoot pufferfish with bubbles to destroy them and earn points.
    • Survive as long as possible to get a high score.
